Bangladesh Olympiadians Club

-

Our organization, "Bangladesh Olympiadians Club", mainly provides various national and international Olympiads information. In addition, this club organizes various events for your practice. At present, there are about 10,000 Olympiadians from different districts of Bangladesh in the community of BOC. Our goal is to inform about various national and international Olympiads and make the students interested in that.
